Evolving an Established Communications Brand

Welcomm Communications

In my role as in-house Digital Designer, I was tasked with supporting the Marketing function as they looked to develop a digital-first identity for their established, award-winning Communications brand.

This multi-channel evolution needed to retain the brand's recognisable, distinct presence in the market, whilst continuing to speak to the needs of its B2B audience.

As the sole design asset within the business, I supported multiple key areas internally, providing bespoke branded Sales and Technical Support documents, assets, user guides and brochures.

Design Areas:

  • Sales and Marketing Collateral
  • Social Media Posts and Visuals
  • Website Content
  • Brand Photography
  • Time-Gated Landing Pages
  • Sales Presentation Decks
  • Sales Proposal Documents
  • Internal Presentation Decks
